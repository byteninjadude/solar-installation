AES Ohio Net Metering & Utility Policies

Local homeowners benefit from AES Ohio's net metering program, which charges a $95 one-time fee to exchange the existing utility meter with a net meter. Their program provides a one-to-one credit system, meaning that the credits you earn for excess electricity are equal to the amount you would otherwise pay for that power at the standard retail rate.

Flexible Solar Financing Solutions

The 30% federal solar tax credit expires after December 31, 2025, allowing homeowners to claim 30% of their total solar installation costs as a federal tax credit. This represents significant savings that won't be available after 2025.
